Keynote Speaker

Nicole Larue

AW 204 11:30-1PM

Nicole LaRue is an illustrator, designer, crafter, and author, who has done work for many companies like Chronicle Books, Oxford University Press, Madison Park Greetings, Tiny Prints, Modern Twist, Hint Creative, American Crafts and most recently the Women's March on Washington campaign. A born creator with a passion for her work, come join us at 1 pm in AW 204 to hear Nicole talk about her work and experience in the industry.

Curious about Nicole? Check out her website, you won’t regret it.
